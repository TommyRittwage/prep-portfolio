ABOUT ME

Though I went to highschool in Los Angeles, I spent much of my formative youth in Chicago. After moving to Los Angeles I became very ill and eventually developed type-1 diabetes, which led to a lifelong pursuit of health and wellness.

In highschool I played guitar or skateboarded with friends during most of my after-school time. Unsure of what I wanted to do later in life, I went to college and studied what interested me: business and psychology.

I currently live in Marina del Rey and work in marketing and circulation for a pair of filmmaking & animation magazines, but I hope to transition to web devolpment when the publications move to wordpress.
